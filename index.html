Data Protection for Hair Professionals

Understand your responsibilities for safeguarding client information and applying data protection principles in your salon.

What is Data Protection?

Data protection means keeping personal information safe, accurate, and used fairly. For hair professionals, this often includes client contact details, appointment records, health information (e.g., allergies), and payment details.

In the UK, the Data Protection Act 2018 and UK GDPR set out how businesses must collect, store, and use personal data responsibly.
