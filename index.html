Legislation

The Data Protection Act 2018 and UK GDPR require salons to:

  • Collect personal data fairly and lawfully.
  • Only use data for specified purposes.
  • Keep it accurate and up to date.
  • Store it securely and only as long as needed.
  • Respect client rights such as access and deletion.
